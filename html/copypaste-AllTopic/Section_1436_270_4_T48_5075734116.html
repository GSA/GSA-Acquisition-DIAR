1436.270-4 Part III - Documents, exhibits and other attachments.

For Section J, List of documents, exhibits, and other attachments, include wage determinations (See FAR 22.404), SF-24 - Bid Bond (See FAR 28.101), and other attachments by listing the title, date and number for each document.
